## Notes on API pricing
For Anthropic API and OpenAI API direct users, the audit engine cannot calculate exact
monthly spend without knowing their token usage. The form asks for their self-reported
monthly spend, and the engine compares that against equivalent flat-plan pricing to flag
potential savings in either direction.
